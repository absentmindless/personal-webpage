ESE 112

Recruited to help design a new freshman electromagnetics class, wrote labs and designed experiments.

Co-wrote Labs 1-6 for the class and designed the experiments within them, available at https://ese112.seas.upenn.edu/labs/. Videos for some of the experiments are available here.
